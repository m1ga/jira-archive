We realized that it is a issue to set the config when the core packages are not yet installed. As a workaround, can you copy the below snippet (replace with your proxy server) in to your [HOME_DIR]\.appcelerator\appc-cli.json (for ex: C:\Users\<user>\.appcelerator\appc-cli.json). 
       {
     "proxyServer": "http://proxy.com"
   }
