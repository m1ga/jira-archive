Description

A map annotation allows left and right buttons to be specified. In 3.4.1, one could expand the annotation by clicking on the pin and then click on the left or right buttons to trigger events (for navigating to the details of the place, for example). In 4.1.0, the click events are not firing. Please see the attached app.js. This bug is a showstopper. We cannot deploy our app to the store without fixing this bug. A quick fix or a workaround is greatly appreciated.
