  1. Ray Kahn 2015-09-20 I found a solution to my problem. Here is what I have found out: When I ran "ps -aux | grep appc" I found hundreds of processes running. I restarted my mac as a quick way to kill all the appc processes. I ran my app again in studio and on the third try the simulator appeared and I am now back to testing my app. This is not a permanent solution.
