  1. Mostafizur Rahman 2016-02-28 Hello, To disable the plugins . Here is how: Go to Help -> Installation Details. Click the Plug-ins tabs to find out what you want to remove. Every feature or plugin has a Feature ID, shown in a column of the same name. This is just the name of its JAR file. Close Appcelerator Studio. Go to the directory where Appcelerator Studio is installed. To remove a plugins, go to the plugins directory. Delete the directory or JAR file. Restart Appcelerator Studio.
