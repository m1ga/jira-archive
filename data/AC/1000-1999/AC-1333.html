Description

Steps to Reproduce

Launch Studio 4.0.0, wait until 'Dashboard' is loaded. Note in upper right corner of view a 'Sign In' button is available.

Actual Result

Launch Studio 4.0.0, wait until 'Dashboard' is loaded. Note in upper right corner of view a 'Sign In' button is available.

Expected Result

Launch Studio, wait until 'Dashboard' is loaded. Note in upper right corner of view a 'Sign In' button should be replaced with 'Sign Out' button. Implication is that Studio 4.0.0 has performed a 'single sign on' to dashboard and the user would not have to enter credentials again to get into dashboard.
