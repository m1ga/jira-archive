  3. chanakya 2015-08-04 Added and updated required files in mac and windows(in java_1.6). Now it is working fine for me in both mac and windows. *Steps:* 1.Download bouncy castle jar here: http://www.bouncycastle.org/download/bcprov-jdk15on-152.jar 2.Copy jar file to: /System/Library/Java/JavaVirtualMachines/1.6.0.jdk/Contents/Home/lib/ext/ 3.Edit: /System/Library/Java/JavaVirtualMachines/1.6.0.jdk/Contents/Home/lib/security/java.security 4.Add the following line where other security providers are defined: security.provider.11=org.bouncycastle.jce.provider.BouncyCastleProvider 5.Restart Titanium Studio 6.Login 7.Success
