  1. Daniel Sefton 2013-04-06 Hi Markus, We do not support adding web views to scroll views. From Apple's official documentation: {quote}Important: You should not embed UIWebView or UITableView objects in UIScrollView objects. If you do so, unexpected behavior can result because touch events for the two objects can be mixed up and wrongly handled.{quote}
