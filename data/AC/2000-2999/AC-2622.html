  1. Carter Lathrop 2013-07-08 Hello Chandira, Is your computer working behind a proxy? If you are trying to login to titanium studio from an office this is more than likely the case and is the culprit of your issue. If so you need to add the following ports to the firewall's white list https://api.appcelerator.net (ports 80 and 443) Used for login http://studio.appcelerator.com (ports 80 and 443) Used for the dashboard http://download.appcelerator.com (ports 80 and 443) Used for software updates This should allow you to login without a problem. Please let me know how this turns out for you. Regards, Carter
