  3. Federico Casali 2012-08-11 Hi Tadashi, I've reproduced your issue while installing on device, my fault before. I have made some research and I found out that this is actually not a Titanium bug, but it's due to the following fact, also explained here http://docs.python.org/howto/unicode.html under the section 'Python 2.x’s Unicode Support' Basically all the argument of the unicode constructor should be 8-bit strings. In our case, ASCII encoding it's used for the conversion and what's happening is that a character greater than 127 is treated as an error. Thus, we cannot support this string character in the provided sample. Regards, Federico
