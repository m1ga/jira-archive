  2. Vishal Duggal 2013-09-17 I am assuming you mean the tintColor for the UITabBar, which is exposed through the Ti.UI.TabGroup.tabsTintColor property. Ti.UI.TabGroup.tabsBackgroundColor maps to UITabBar.barTintColor on iOS7 and above and UITabBar.tintColor on older versions.
