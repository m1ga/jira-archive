  1. Sharif AbuDarda 2016-04-23 Hello, I have tested the issue you reported. I see you are using "keyboardType="Titanium.UI.KEYBOARD_ASCII"". Titanium.UI.KEYBOARD_ASCII is deprecated. http://docs.appcelerator.com/platform/latest/#!/api/Titanium.UI-property-KEYBOARD_ASCII. Use Titanium.UI.KEYBOARD_DEFAULT instead. Also, you are calling editable="true" twice in the textfield. Regards, Sharif.
  2. Motiur Rahman 2016-04-23 Hello Rui, There are two "editable" property on your first textfield, so remove one and make it editable="false". Then let us know how it goes. 
       <TextField id="username" borderStyle="Ti.UI.INPUT_BORDERSTYLE_ROUNDED" keyboardType="Titanium.UI.KEYBOARD_TYPE_ASCII" editable="false" class="TextBox2"></TextField>
    Thanks.
