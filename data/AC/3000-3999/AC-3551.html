Comments

  1. Nazmus Salahin 2016-04-27 Hello, Thanks for your query. Starting with Alloy 1.8 the location of your /i18n and /platform source directories for Alloy has changed. These used to live in your project root, just like they do for classic Titanium projects. They are now expected to be at /app/i18n and /app/platform. When you build your app, Alloy will generate the root /i18n and /platform directories, just like it generates the /Resources directory. Any manual change to files in these directories will be lost during the next build. [Blog Link](http://www.appcelerator.com/blog/2016/03/alloy-1-8-relocates-i18n-and-platform-directories/) Thanks
