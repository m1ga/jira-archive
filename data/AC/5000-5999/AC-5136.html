  1. Motiur Rahman 2017-08-12 Hello [~jackSparrow], It's not a bug. Item templates only support the following view classes: Titanium.UI.ActivityIndicator Titanium.UI.Button Titanium.UI.ImageView Titanium.UI.Label Titanium.UI.ProgressBar Titanium.UI.Slider Titanium.UI.Switch Titanium.UI.TextArea Titanium.UI.TextField Please take a look following doc links - http://docs.appcelerator.com/platform/latest/#!/guide/ListViews-section-src-37521650_ListViews-ItemTemplate Hope this helps
