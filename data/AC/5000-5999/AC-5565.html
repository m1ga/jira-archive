  5. Joshua Quick 2018-02-05 [~bpawar], I'm able to reproduce it now. Thanks. Right, the issue is that the ScrollView width ignores Ti.UI.SIZE and does a FILL instead. This issue was introduced when we added "RefreshControl" support to ScrollView. It's technically a bug on Google's end where their Java "SwipeRefreshLayout" class ignores the "WRAP_CONTENT" setting, which is the Android equivalent of our Ti.UI.SIZE setting. We'll have to work-around this issue and calculate it ourselves.
