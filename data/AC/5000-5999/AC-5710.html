Description

When changing a label from fontWeight normal to fontWeight bold the text sometimes becomes prematurely ellipsed. To recreate, run the attached project and click the 'Locations' label. The text becomes ellipsed despite having room to grow. Clicking another label changes the text back to normal and no longer ellipses. This appears to only be an issue on iOS. Android does not prematurely ellipse.
