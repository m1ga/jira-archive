[ERROR] :  ** BUILD FAILED **
[ERROR] :  The following build commands failed:
[ERROR] :  	Ld /Users/name/Documents/Appcelerator_Studio_Workspace/AppName/build/iphone/build/Products/Debug-iphonesimulator/AppName.app/AppName normal x86_64
[ERROR] :  (1 failure)
