Ti.Geolocation.requestLocationPermissions(Ti.Geolocation.AUTHORIZATION_WHEN_IN_USE, function(e) {

  1. Lorenzo Piccinini 2020-10-29 Request of permissions was in the main script (not alloy style) of window generation. Probably it was my mistake but something is changed switching from 9.2.0 to 9.2.1 SDK for iOS. On android instead it didn’t work even with 9.2.0 and earlier and I hadn’t noticed it.
