Description

A popular class of applications starting to show up on mobile devices is the brochure app. They are apps that gives you a virtual and interactive overview of a product. Having a template that creates the scaffolding and then a complete app that has art, effects and flow would be a good start to quickly building one of these apps. For example, http://www.motorauthority.com/news/1073083_mercedes-benz-launches-c-class-brochure-app Clothing, Autos, Kitchen Appliances could be good start points for one of these apps.
