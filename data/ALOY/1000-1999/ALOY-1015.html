  1. Malcolm Hollingsworth 2014-05-14 Ti.include should be deprecated and removed as soon as possible. It was a solution to a problem that has long been solved. If you are wanting to include large sections of code in-line then re-think and convert them to CommonJS. Problem solved and much better code in your app.
