Description

The new /DefaultIcon.png which has platform specific versions at /DefaultIcon-<platform>.png should be theme-able via Alloy. We probably need a similar approach as with i18n and platform since we can't overwrite the DefaultIcon.png in the project root, so we have to do it at build-time. - https://github.com/appcelerator/alloy/pull/662/files#diff-ddbdb7300fa15590afdfee9246ac61fcR327 - https://github.com/appcelerator/alloy/pull/662/files#diff-6487ccfa4909144bbc33134e4d85bdddR159 Kindly pointed out by [~jasonkneen]: https://twitter.com/jasonkneen/status/654326054488834048
