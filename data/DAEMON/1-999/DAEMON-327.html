Description

Cleaning a Titanium project usually is just a matter of nuking all of the files in the project's "build" directory. However, there are at least one Titanium CLI plugin that depends on the clean command hook: https://github.com/appcelerator/alloy/blob/master/hooks/deepclean.js. For time sake and to reduce breakage, the "clean" command will just invoke the Titanium CLI v5.
