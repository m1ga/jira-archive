Description

Views > ImageViews > Animated: Animation gets paused after 4-5 frames and resume occurs leaving 1 frame in between. This is not regression. This issue occurs in 2.1.0 Steps to Reproduce: 1. Go to Kitchensink> BaseUI> Views> Imageviews> Animated. 2. Click the pause button. 3. Click resume. Actual: 2. Animation gets paused after 4-5 frames. 3. Animation resumes leaving 1 frame in between. Expected: Pause and resume should take place immediately after button click.
