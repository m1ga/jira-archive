Description

Description: Two labels disappear after changing device mode to landscape in "Horizontal Layout". This occurs only on LG VS910 4G (2.3.6) phone. Steps: 1. Run Refactored KitchenSink in portrait mode. 2. In "Base UI" module press on "Horizontal Layout". 3. After three labels are displayed turn device on landscape mode. Expected results: Three labels visible on the screen. Actual results: Only third label is visible.
