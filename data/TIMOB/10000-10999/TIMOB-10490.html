Description

Attached are the app.js and sample movie ( to be copied under resources). 1. Run the app and click the 'preview movie' button. 2. Start the movie, switch to landscape and wait for the movie to finish. 3. Once the playback is ended, switch back to portrait mode and tap the screen to bring up the controls. 4. Click 'enable full screen' button ( available in top right hand corner of the screen) and then click 'Done' button. 5. The video player will be displaced off the center and controls are placed beyond the lower edge of the screen.
