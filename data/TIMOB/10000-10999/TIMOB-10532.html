Description

Description: On Base UI> Views> Scroll Views> "Scrolling Zoom+Pinch" image changes location during device rotation. It moves from center to left top corner. This is not a regression. The behavior occurs on 2.1.1 as well. Steps: 1.Open "Scrolling Zoom+Pinch" in portrait mode. 2.Minimize the image which is located in the center of the screen. 3.Rotate device in to landscape mode. Expected results: Image remains in the center of the page. Actual results: Image jumps in to left top conner of the page.
