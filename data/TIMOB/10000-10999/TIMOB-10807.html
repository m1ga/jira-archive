Description

ScrollView on iOS doesn't generate 'postlayout' events when scrolled or zoomed, nor are the children's rect and size properties updated. This may be unavoidable--in which case, we should document that the postlayout, rect, and size properties are of limited utility for the children of ScrollViews.
