Description

When setting a Ti.UI.Label text as an empty string ('' or "") and adding it to a view on iOS 6, the app crashes with a seg fault. This works on iOS 5. Sample code: var win = Ti.UI.createWindow(); var label = Ti.UI.createLabel({ text: '' }); win.add(label); win.show();
