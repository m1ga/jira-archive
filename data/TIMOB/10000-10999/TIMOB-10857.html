Description

Steps to reproduce: 1. Run KS->Controls->Activity Indicator on Galaxy Nexus (4.0.4) with SDK 3.0.0. 2. Click "Show(Basic)". A small grey spinning wheel shows. On 2.x / 3.x devices, it is a small white wheel. 3. Click "Show Indicator (Dark)". A small grey spinning wheel shows. On 2.x / 3.x devices, it is a small black wheel. 4. Click "Show Indicator (BIG)". A big grey spinning wheel shows. On 2.x / 3.x devices, it is a big white wheel. 5. Click "Show Indicator (BIG_Dark)". A big grey spinning wheel shows. On 2.x / 3.x devices, it is a big black wheel. Not sure if this is a Titanium bug or a native android issue.
