Description

Play sound and click hardware back button does not kill the sound. This is not regression since the issue occurs in 2.1.2 as well. Steps to reproduce: 1. Go to Kitchensink> Phone> Sound> local 2. Click play. Sound starts playing. 3. set looping true. 4. Click hardware back button. 5. Again click local. 6. Again click hardware back button. Actual: 4. Sound keeps playing. 5. Sound stops playing. 6. Sound starts playing. Expected: 4. Sound should stop playing. 5. Sound should not play unless started. 6. Sound should not play.
