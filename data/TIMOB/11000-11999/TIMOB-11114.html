  3. Vishal Duggal 2012-10-04 IOS 6 has changed the way View Controllers interact with device orientation. The full screen video player is its own View Controller and outside the control of the Window that contains the video player. On the iPhone idiom any View Controller by default supports PORTRAIT, LANDSCAPE_LEFT and LANDSCAPE_RIGHT orientations. The windows orientation modes do not restrict the orientations of system view controllers that appear on top of it.
