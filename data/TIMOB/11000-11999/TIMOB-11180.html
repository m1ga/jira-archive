Description

Description: Keyboard on "Text Field" page disappears after device has been rotated couple of times. Steps: 1. Launch KitchenSink. 2. Go to Controls> Text Field> Toolbar. 3. Press "Focus to see keyboard w/toolbar". 4. Click on the toolbar's textfield to activate it. 5. Rotate device fast multiple times (10-17). Expected results: Keyboard remains on the screen. Actual results: Keyboard disappears. Note: on iPhone version 4.3.5 keyboard remains on the screen, but during rotation keyboard's toolbar "jumps".
