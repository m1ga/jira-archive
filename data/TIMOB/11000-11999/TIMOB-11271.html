Description

Text field shows bounce effect if enabled when it's half visible on the screen. This is not regression since the issue occurs as far as 2.1.2 GA Steps to reproduce: 1. Go to Kitchensink> Controls> TextField in scrollview. 2. Scroll up till the middle text field is half visible. 3. Click on it to enable the textfield. Actual: Textfield bounces to come down and then go up. It occurs only on device, not on simulator. Expected: The textfield should not bounce.
