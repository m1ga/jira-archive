Description

Buttons do not lose the effect of last click unless Editable:Enable and Editable:Disable button is clicked. This is not regression since the issue occurs as far as 2.1.2 Steps to reproduce: 1. Go to Kitchensink> Controls> Text Area> AutoLink. All links are enabled by default. 2. Click 'Detect address' button. 3. Click Editable:Enable and then Editable:Disabled button. Actual: 2. Nothing happens. 3. Address appears as selected. Expected: Buttons should work without Editable:Enable and Editable:Disabled button click.
