Description

Controls are not rendered correctly if second animation is started before the completion of first one. This is not regression since the issue occurs in 2.1.3GA as well. Steps to reproduce: 1. Go to Kitchensink> BaseUI> Animation> windows. 2. Click on Animation(slide). 3. Before the slide animation gets complete, Click on Animation(custom). Actual: Controls on screen are not rendered properly after the animation gets finished. Expected: Controls should render properly after animation gets completed.
