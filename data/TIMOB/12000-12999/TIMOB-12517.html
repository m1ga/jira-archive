Description

Tabbar disappears with different animations in different devices. This is not regression since the issue occurs on 3.0.0 GA as well. Steps to reproduce: 1. Install Kitchensink on iPhone3GS(v5.0.1) and iPhone5(v 6.0) 2. Go to BaseUI > Window(standalone) > Open tab w/o animation. Actual: When click on open tab w/o animation on iPhone3GS, the tab bar disappears with bottom up animation whereas on iPhone5, tab bar disappears with slide animation. Expected: Same behavior should be observed on both devices.
