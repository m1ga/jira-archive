Comments

  1. Chris Barber 2013-06-05 This can be done today by just doing a iOS Device build. The ipa file is sitting in the build/iphone/build/Debug-iphoneos/ directory. The only problem is if you do a device build, it will generate the ipa and initiate an iTunes sync. Due to a bug, the --build-only flag will unfortunately skip the ipa generation. That will be fixed with TIMOB-13663. So in the meantime, you'll have to deal with iTunes launching or copy the "iphone/cli/hooks/install.js" hook and modify it to build the ipa, but not initiate the iTunes sync. Otherwise wait until TIMOB-13663 is fixed and use a master build or wait until 3.2 ships. :)
