Description

Client App which is running fine on devices running Android version except 4.2.x. Trying to install the app on android version 4.2.x device, crashes the app after some time. **Steps to reproduce** On the Android 4.2.x phone we launch the app and then do something like the following: 1) Scroll all the way down the list 2) Tap on Tab 2 3) Scroll down 4) Repeat steps 2-3 tapping the other tabs After a few tab changes (~6), the app starts to slow down, clic clicking from tab to tab is not responsive and eventually app would crash.
