Go to Kitchensink> controls> Picker> Countdown Picker 2. Select any time(say 2 hrs 30 mins). Press start. 3. press stop and again press start Actual: 2. Countdown starts from 9000 secs. 3. Time is reset to 0hrs 2 mins and countdown starts from 180 secs. Expected: Time should reset first time also and countdown should start from 180 secs
