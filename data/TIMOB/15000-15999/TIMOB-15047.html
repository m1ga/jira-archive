Description

Not a Regression. Different behaviour of MODAL_PRESENTATION_FORMSHEET on iphone and ipad. Same happens on ipad but on iphone the whole screen gets covered. Steps to reproduce: 1)KitchenSink>BaseUI>Modal Windows 2)Choose combination of any animation along with formsheet option.
