Description

In Android, HttpClient does not share cookies with WebView. Need to expose the cookie management methods so developers can manage their cookies. Reference: http://developer.android.com/reference/android/webkit/CookieManager.html http://developer.android.com/reference/org/apache/http/client/CookieStore.html http://developer.android.com/reference/org/apache/http/impl/cookie/BasicClientCookie.html
