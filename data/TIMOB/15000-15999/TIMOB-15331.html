Description

With the advent of many low-cost Android Devices (especially tablets) based on MIPS architecture we should expect a lot of near future demand for apps compatible with those devices (take a look for instance on the Ainol Novo 7). As of todays (Ti SDK 3.1.3.GA), Titanium only supports arm, armv7a and x86 ABIs for Android. We should start thinking about MIPS as soon as possible. Some info: http://www.androidcentral.com/mips-announces-125-jelly-bean-tablet http://liliputing.com/2012/07/android-4-1-coming-soon-to-mips-based-devices.html http://www.mipsdroid.com/ http://phandroid.com/2012/07/31/smart-tab-1-is-a-mips-based-jelly-bean-running-tablet-with-low-125-price-tag/
