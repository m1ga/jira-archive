Description

Customer developed a project from the CLI and the .project file was missing these values: com.appcelerator.titanium.mobile.nature com.aptana.projects.webnature Once we added them back we could enable services for the app in the proper org. Without the values, the app shows it isn't part of any org and won't properly link to Crittercism and SOASTA.
