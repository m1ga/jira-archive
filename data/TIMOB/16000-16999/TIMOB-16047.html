Description

var win = Titanium.UI.createWindow({backgroundColor:'#ccc'});
var textField = Ti.UI.createTextField({
  width: 250, top: 100,
  backgroundColor: 'white'
});
win.add(textField);
win.open();
1. Create app with the above code. 2. Enter a word and allow the phone to auto-complete for you 3. Press space and enter another word NOTE: (Good idea to keep on going til the you have scrolled off screen)

Actual:

A whitespace will be appended to the beginning of the text field.

Expected:

No leading whitespace
