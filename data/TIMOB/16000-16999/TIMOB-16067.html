  16. Chris Barber 2014-01-17 [~erick] Can you see your device when you run adb devices? If yes, then run ti info -t android and you should see your device listed under Connected Android Devices. If you do not see your device under adb devices, then run adb kill-server and try again. If your device shows up in adb devices but not in ti info -t android, then ensure Titanium has the correct path to the Android SDK by running titanium setup android and try the steps above again. Also, you have installed the Android USB driver, right? It's in C:\path\to\android-sdk\extras\google\usb_driver.
