Description

Steps: 1. Create app (Alloy or classic) that displays a Switch control with SWITCH_STYLE_CHECKBOX style, e.g: 
<Alloy>
    <Window class="container">
        <Switch id="basicSwitch2" 
            title="Option name" 
            style="Titanium.UI.Android.SWITCH_STYLE_CHECKBOX" />
    </Window>
</Alloy>
2. Build for Android. Result: Checkbox label is white by default, so doesn't appear on white background. Expected: Checkbox label is black by default, like most Titanium label/text objects. Found the solution from a StackOverflow poster who encountered same problem: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/14171078/title-is-not-displayed-in-a-checkbox-in-titanium
