Class Overview

An Equalizer is used to alter the frequency response of a particular music source or of the main output mix. An application creates an Equalizer object to instantiate and control an Equalizer engine in the audio framework. The application can either simply use predefined presets or have a more precise control of the gain in each frequency band controlled by the equalizer.
