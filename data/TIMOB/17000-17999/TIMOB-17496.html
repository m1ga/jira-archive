Description

When the Electric Company app is first opened, there is a scrollable view nested within a ScrollView, and the parent ScrollView is unresponsive to touches until the ScrollableView has been swiped. Only then can you actually scroll to the rest of the ScrollView. Steps to Reproduce: -Open Electric Company app -Attempt to scroll vertically Actual Result: -View fails to respond Expected Result: -View scrolls properly Additional Notes: Works as expected with Ti SDK 3.2.2 Electric Company app is available here: https://www.dropbox.com/s/g0g3ffigsr9nhbp/Electric%20Company.zip
