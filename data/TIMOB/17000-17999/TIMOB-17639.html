    options: ['a', 'b']

});

Comments

  1. Steven van Loef 2014-09-08 A work around (documented way http://docs.appcelerator.com/platform/latest/#!/api/Titanium.UI.OptionDialog-method-show) is to specify a view and rect when showing the dialog: 
       Ti.UI.createOptionDialog({
       options: ['a', 'b']
   }).show({view:win, rect:{width:1, height:1, x: 100, y: 200});
    For rect only x and y are used (see http://docs.appcelerator.com/platform/latest/#!/api/showParams).
  2. Fokke Zandbergen 2014-09-09 [~ludolphus] you are right, (it doesn't even need rect in order to work), but of course - as documented - it should also work without and then show centered. I guess the title of the ticket should be *iOS8: Ti.UI.OptionDialog.show() doesn't work without view-property on iPad*
