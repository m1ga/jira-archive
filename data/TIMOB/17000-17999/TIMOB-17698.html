Description

Isue description

If the user rotates the device immediately when launching image capture then there are black bars present and the video is rotated.

Steps to reproduce

Launch sample and put the app in landscape orientation.

Click launch image capture, and immediately rotate it to portrait mode.

Notice that there are black bars present and that the image capture is rotated. This is despite setting the autorotate property to false when calling showCamera.

Expected

The image capture view is full screen, just how it launches if the user does not rotate the device immediately upon launching image capture.
