Description

Description:

The iPhone 6+ shares many similarities with iPad in regards to orientation. Currently orientation is controlled with the iPhone tiapp.xml tag, so is not reaching it's full screen real estate potential. The suggested improvement is to treat the 6+ orientation either separately or with more finite control for orientation to distinguish it from iPhone <=6.

Steps to reproduce:

1) Compare native applications run on iPhone 6, iPhone 6+ and iPad

Result:

Most applications that do not rotate on iPhone 6 will rotate on iPhone 6+. In some cases, a master/detail view similar to iPad is shown on iPhone 6+ while the iPhone 6 retains a simpler view. Also, the main UI rotates on iPad and iPhone 6+ but does not on iPhone 6 See the screen shots for comparison
