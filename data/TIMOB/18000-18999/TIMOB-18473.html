Description

This is a regression. The issue is not seen with 3.5.1.GA

Link to download QE Automation app: https://www.dropbox.com/s/gstz67dw6pbs6jj/QE%20Automator.zip?dl=0

Description:

1. Build the QE automation app on an android device( I have tried on android 5.0 & android 4.4.2). 2. Launch the app. 3. Scroll the listview. 4. Click on any test.

Actual result:

1. You will see some of the information & play buttons are missing for some tests. 2. You will see that the app crashes(some tests might launch but finally it will crash). NOTE: Please refer the attached logs on android 5.0 & android 4.4.2 device for reference. We do not see a crash on android 4.4.2 device but just out of memory errors.

Expected Result:

1. The app should not crash when built with sdk 3.6.0.v20150127082526
