Description

In one of my App Templates (https://github.com/ricardoalcocer/AlloyAppTemplate) there's NavigationWindow with a RightNavButton that opens a child window. When compiled with 3.5.0.GA, that button only opens the child window two times in a row. To reproduce, clone the repo, make sure you set sdk-version to 3.5.0.GA on tiapp.xml. When the app runs, click on the + button, go back, click again, go back, click again.
