Description

Google has announced that they will be end of lifing support for ADT by the end of the year. This requires that everyone moves over to Android Studio. This impacts by Titanium SDK and Android module development. More information: http://android-developers.blogspot.com/2015/06/an-update-on-eclipse-android-developer.html
