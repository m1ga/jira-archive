  6. Chris Barber 2015-09-10 [~timpoulsen] The MobileDevice error 0xe8000005 is unknown. I have no idea what triggers this. Looking at your logs in your previous comment, I see the symlink reference. You can try to build with \-\-force\-copy and see if that fixes it. Recently I "fixed" something where iTunes artwork was being copied for device and dist-appstore builds, but really only needed to be copied for dist-adhoc builds. I also remove iTunesArtwork from the symlink blacklist (https://github.com/appcelerator/titanium_mobile/commit/7639da56fdcbbb31c9188d15c70c0da363689cd0#diff-f430483233aa01af5b10df8390f9635dL3946). The reason why is the copyResources() build step copies from the Resources directory, not the project root, so you'll never copy an iTunesArtwork, so there's no reason to try to ignore it. The latest code does not symlink iTunesArtwork anyways.
