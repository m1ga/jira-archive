  3. Lokesh Choudhary 2018-03-26 [~amukherjee], [~emerriman], This does not appear to be an issue anymore. I checked with latest 7.1.0 GA components by creating an intent filter so that whenever the URI is accessed android gives an option to open the app. 
       <intent-filter>
           <action android:name="android.intent.action.VIEW" />
           <category android:name="android.intent.category.DEFAULT" />
           <category android:name="android.intent.category.BROWSABLE" />
           <!-- Accepts URIs that begin with "http://www.example.com.com/hello” -->
           <data android:scheme="http"
                 android:host="www.example.com"
                 android:pathPrefix="/hello" />
           <!-- note that the leading "/" is required for pathPrefix-->
   </intent-filter>
