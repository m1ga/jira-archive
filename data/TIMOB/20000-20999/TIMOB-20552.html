Description

In Titanium 5.1.0 we implemented textinput behavior for iOS notifications with TIMOB-19184. Android just [announced](http://docs.appcelerator.com/platform/latest/#!/api/Titanium.App.iOS-property-USER_NOTIFICATION_BEHAVIOR_TEXTINPUT) a similar features for Android N.
