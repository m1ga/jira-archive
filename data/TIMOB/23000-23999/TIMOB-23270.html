  1. Chee Kiat Ng 2016-04-25 This is not possible natively. (There maybe a way to hack around it but it'll be ugly. There may be also a way to do it via html but it's beyond our sdk scope). You can only support returnKeyType for native textfields. There's no control over the content of a UIWebView interacting with any native components.
