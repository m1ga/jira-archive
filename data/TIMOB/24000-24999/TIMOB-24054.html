  2. Chris Barber 2016-10-20 [~cng] I'm not sure about Alloy, but you cannot name your app "modules" because the compiled executable "modules" would collide with the "modules" directory that stores module assets. The iOS build has a list of blacklisted names that are disallowed app names and resources in the root of the Resources or platform-specific Resources directory. These blacklisted names either collide with a Titanium resource or are known red flags with Apple. From https://github.com/appcelerator/titanium_mobile/blob/master/iphone/cli/commands/_build.js#L72-L85: * contents * resources * plugins * watch * _codesignature * embedded.mobileprovision * info.plist * pkginfo * assets.car * modules * LaunchScreen.storyboardc * hyperloop
