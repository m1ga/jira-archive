Description

A long time ago Google released the [Android Design Support library](http://android-developers.blogspot.com.au/2015/05/android-design-support-library.html). It allows the usage of Material Design components in all Android 2.1 or higher devices. It's been more than two years since Material Design was released and we still don't have access to the components that make up the Android design, and the only way to solve that as of today is trying to replicate them with other Titanium components. On the other hand, Titanium received updates to support new iOS features within weeks or even days over the past few years.
