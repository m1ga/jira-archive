Frankie Merzadyan 2017-04-26 Marking as resolved because: In Network module - the [broadcast receiver](https://github.com/appcelerator/titanium_mobile/blob/master/android/modules/network/src/java/ti/modules/titanium/network/TiNetworkListener.java#L43) still works in API >= 24 as we [register it dynamically](https://github.com/appcelerator/titanium_mobile/blob/master/android/modules/network/src/java/ti/modules/titanium/network/TiNetworkListener.java#L43). Leaving Network related files as is. We do not listen or have receivers for ACTION_NEW_PICTURE or ACTION_NEW_VIDEO broadcasts. Any other changes in Android 7 regarding [background optimizations](https://developer.android.com/topic/performance/background-optimization.html), do not apply to us like this example.
