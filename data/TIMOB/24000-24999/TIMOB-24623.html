Description

To reduce maintenance work we should drop the support for CocoaPods 0.39 and below in Hyperloop projects. CocoaPods 1.0 is almost around for a year now so it should be safe to move forward and only support this version, especially after http://blog.cocoapods.org/Sharding/.
