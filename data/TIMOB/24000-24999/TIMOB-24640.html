  2. Chris Barber 2017-05-02 This is a duplicate of TIMOB-24362. PackageApplication was a command line tool that shipped with Xcode, but was removed in Xcode 8.3. In Titanium SDK 6.0.2.GA we removed the dependency on PackageApplication. Since you are using 6.0.3.GA, you should be just fine. I recommend two things: 1) upgrade to 6.0.4.GA and 2) clean your project, then build.
