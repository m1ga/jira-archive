  4. Hans Kn√∂chel 2017-08-27 Native Android does not support opening local web-views, this is a platform limitation. Native discussions (like [here](https://stackoverflow.com/a/9434924/5537752)) suggest Google Drive open to it or open it as an Intent (supported by Titanium as well, more native experience). Other options are JS-libraries like pdf.js, but the web-view itself will not be able to handle PDF's until Android itself can.
