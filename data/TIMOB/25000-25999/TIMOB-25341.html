  3. Jan Vennemann 2017-09-27 The Hyperloop module is not open source yet. Unfortunately, the only solution for this until Hyperloop 2.2.0 is out, is to stick to Xcode 8.3.3. Apple made a major version bump for the included clang compiler to 4.0 with Xcode 9, which is causing some troubles during metabase generation. We are working hard to include a fix for this in Hyperloop 2.2.0 and get that out asap!
