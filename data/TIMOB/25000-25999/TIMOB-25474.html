  8. Joshua Quick 2018-02-21 Okay. We're able to reproduce it now. The issue is with scrolling a child "horizontal" ScrollView within a parent "vertical" ScrollView. All other nested child views scroll as expected (ie: vertical ScrollViews/TableView/ListView within a vertical ScrollView scroll fine). I've written this up as a separate ticket to better clarify the issue here: [TIMOB-25792] I've also posted a work-around in that same ticket.
