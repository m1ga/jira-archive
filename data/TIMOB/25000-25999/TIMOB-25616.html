Description

In TIMOB-25017, we've been seeing that the persistent in app properties are erased every time on the installation. We found that it is because WinAppDeployCmd fails when same version of the app is already installed, and then Titanium tries to uninstall existing app in order to complete the installation. We can suppress the uninstallation by bumping up the version in the tiapp.xml everytime on deployment, but sometimes uninstallation can be done unintentionally when you forget the bump. We might want a new option to suppress unintentional uninstallation by forcing --forceUnInstall or something explicitly.
