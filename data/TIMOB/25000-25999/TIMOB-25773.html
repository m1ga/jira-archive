  1. Joshua Quick 2018-02-13 Hmm... interesting. It's not just a space issue, because a project folder named "Path Test" builds fine with 7.0.2.GA. However, a folder name with a space followed by a dash (ie: " -") does cause a build failure. It appears that the gradle ProGuard step is attempting to parse a command line argument from it when it shouldn't. We'll look into it.
