  1. Chris Barber 2020-01-30 There seems to be a couple Node.js-based solutions these days. Here are two of them: Sharp https://www.npmjs.com/package/sharp Includes native C++ bindings, but it does support prebuilds, so it will work for current Node.js releases (not future ones). Jimp https://www.npmjs.com/package/jimp Pure JavaScript implementation, but may have poor performance compared to the existing resizer.jar or Sharp, especially if resizing many files.
