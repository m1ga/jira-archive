Comments

  1. Kota Iguchi 2018-10-30 KEYBOARD_TYPE_NUMBERS_PUNCTUATION is already supported as of 6.2.0.GA. Note that this only works for software keyboard for Windows 10 mobile device as far as I can see. 
       var win = Ti.UI.createWindow({
       backgroundColor: 'green'
   });
   
   var textfield = Ti.UI.createTextField({
       keyboardType: Ti.UI.KEYBOARD_TYPE_NUMBERS_PUNCTUATION,
       width: '80%', height: 10
   });
   
   win.add(textfield);
   win.open();
