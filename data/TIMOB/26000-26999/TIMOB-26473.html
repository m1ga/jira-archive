Description

I am on 7.4.0GA. Running this code: 
Ti.API.info("displayCaps-platform-H:" + Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformHeight + ", W:" + Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth + ", " + Ti.Platform.displayCaps.density);
 Returns this for these simulators (iOS 12.0): iPhone X/XS: displayCaps-platform-H:812, W:375, xhigh iPhone 7 Plus: displayCaps-platform-H:736, W:414, high iPhone XR: displayCaps-platform-H:812, W:375, high iPhone XS Max: displayCaps-platform-H:812, W:375, xhigh I believe the XR & XS Max are both incorrect and should instead return: iPhone XR: displayCaps-platform-H:896, W:414, high iPhone XS Max: displayCaps-platform-H:896, W:414, xhigh
