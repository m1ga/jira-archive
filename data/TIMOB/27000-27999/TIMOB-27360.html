Description

Steps to reproduce:

1. Use the watch app here : https://github.com/appcelerator-developer-relations/appc-sample-watchos2 2. Build it to a watch & iPhone simulator.

Actual result:

1. You will see the app installs on the phone but does not install on the watch.

Expected result:

1. The app should install on both phone & watch.

Comments

Installing an iOS app to the iOS Simulator using Xcode 11's simctl no longer installs the watch apps and thus they must be explicitly installed. To test, I used DEBUG="*,-babel" appc run -p ios --log-level trace --launch-watch-app.
