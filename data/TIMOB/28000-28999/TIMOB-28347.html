Description

After implementing TIMOB-26915, in iOS, every WebView will have it's own cookie management. It will not be in sync with Ti.Network.HTTPClient cookies. In android, it looks [System Cookie](https://docs.appcelerator.com/platform/latest/#!/api/Titanium.Network-method-addSystemCookie) are used for WebView only. I think it can be moved inside WebView to have parity. New WebView cookie APIs - Method - a) webview.addCookie(Titanium.Network.Cookie); b) webview.removeCookie('domain', 'path', 'name'); c) webview.removeAllCookies(); d) webview. removeCookiesForDomain('domain') -> Array(Titanium.Network.Cookie) e) webview.getCookies(''domain', 'path', 'name') -> Array(Titanium.Network.Cookie) f) webview.cookiesForDomain('domain') -> Array(Titanium.Network.Cookie) Property (read-only) - e) webview.allCookies -> Array(Titanium.Network.Cookie)
