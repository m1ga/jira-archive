See: [TIMOB-28456] You can re-add jcenter support via a "build.gradle" (example shown in the above ticket), but it's a ticking time bomb since the jcenter servers will be shutdown in less than a year from now. Most maintained jcenter dependencies have been moved to mavenCentral, but you may have to reference newer versions of those dependencies to access them. Sorry about the confusion.
