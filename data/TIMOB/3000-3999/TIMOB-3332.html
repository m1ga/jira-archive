Description

In Android, a textField with passwordMask: true, looses its mask when the device it rotated to landscape

Step1: open KitchenSink
Step2: Controls > Text Field > The Rest
Step3: click 'Password Mask'
Step4: type a word in the text field
Step5: rotate the device to landscape
Step6: notice the password mask disappears and the text is displayed plainly

Tested On ###

TiSDK 1.6.0, Android 2.3.2, Nexus S
