Description

Description: While testing KitchenSink, Base UI>Views>Dashboard view, clicking a dashboard icon (other than the first two icons) a window opens with a close button. When you click the close button, the button is moved to the upper left hand corner of the window and animates with the window as it closes. Steps to reproduce: 1) Install KitchenSink 1.7.0 on an iPhone or other device 2) Launch Base UI>Views>Dashboard 3) Click an icon other than the first two to open the new window 4) Click the close button Result: The close button moves to the upper left-hand of the window and stays as the animation progresses Expected Result: The close button is removed, then the window animates as it closes Notes:
