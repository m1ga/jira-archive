Description

Description: When testing Base UI>Window NavBar, a rotation change can result in a poorly rendered Nav bar area. Steps to reproduce: 1) Install KS 1.7.0 on iPad 2) Navigate to Base UI>Window NavBar in portrait orientation 3) Tap "bar color" 4) Tap "set bar image" 5) Rotate to landscape (for added fun, then tap "title prompt") Result: Nav bar area rendered poorly Expected Result: Correctly rendered nav bar Notes: OCCURS on 1.6.2 OCCURS on iPad 2
