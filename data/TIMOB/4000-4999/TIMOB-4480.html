Description

Let's say we have a DashboardView that shows 12 items per page, and we have 12 items. If we drag an icon onto Page 2 and then stop editing, that icon is immediately placed back into Page 1. Request is that we allow icons to remain where they're placed, even if the previous page isn't full (this is similar to iOS SpringBoard functionality). This could be implemented, perhaps, by allowing a "page" property on DashboardItems.
