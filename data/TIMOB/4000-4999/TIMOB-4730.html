Comments

  1. Chris Barber 2013-11-09 I'm resolving this because "poor" performance depends on the hardware and type of Android emulator. If you are using a hardware accelerated emulator such as the x86 HAXM Android emulator or Genymotion, things are pretty darn fast. Now that the new Android build system allows you to select a specific Android emulator, it should never launch a useless emulator and it's up to you to configure a fast x86-based emulator.
