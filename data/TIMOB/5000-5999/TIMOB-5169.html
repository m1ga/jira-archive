Description

When moving a scrollable view in iOS, the view follows the finger until a threshold is met that triggers the paging mechanism to show the next/previous "page". In Android, the view does not follow the finger up until the threshold. This ticket exists to track the discussion of the parity difference and the fix if one is agreed upon.
