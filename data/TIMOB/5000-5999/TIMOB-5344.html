Description

Description: While testing KS 172 on iOS 5, Base UI>Views>Coverflow view, I noticed a difference in the animation of the images as they moved from the sides to the center spot in the coverflow. The images on either side of the centered image have a gap between the image edge and the screen edge (in landscape mode). On iOS 4.3.x, the side images move to the center without much change in this gap. On iOS 5, the side image fills the gap between itself and the side of the screen, giving a less-than-smooth appearance to the animation. Steps to Reproduce: 1) Install KS with SDK 1.7.2 onto iOS 5 device 2) Navigate to Base UI>Views>Coverflow view, any of them 3) Switch to device landscape mode 4) Watch the gap between the "on-deck" image and the side of the screen 5) Transition the "on-deck" image to the center location Results: The animation is not smooth and differs from the animation on 4.3.x. Expected results: Smooth animation that matches 4.3.x performance. Note: Does not occur with iOS 4.3x OCCURS with SDK 1.8.0.v20110922105247
