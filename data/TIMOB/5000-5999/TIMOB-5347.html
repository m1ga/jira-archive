Description: While testing KS 172 on iOS 5, Base UI>Window NavBar, I tapped "set bar image" and nothing appear to have happened. After a short investigation it was apparent that the button was working, but the image was appearing behind the navbar. Steps to Reproduce: 1) Install KS with SDK 1.7.2 onto iOS 5 device 2) Navigate to Base UI>Window NavBar 3) Press "set bar image" to see the bar image: 1) Relaunch test 2) Press "title prompt" button 3) Press "set bar image" 4) Press "title prompt" button again Results: No navbar image appears. Expected results: Navbar image changes to genuine simulated wood grain Note: Does not occur with iOS 4.3x OCCURS with SDK 1.8.0.v20110922105247
