Description

Description: When launching Kitchen sink, on any of the main windows, the navbar does not resize when rotating the device to landscape then back to portrait. This did not occur with 1.7.2 GA or 1.7.3 GA, but does also occur with with 1.8.0. Steps to reproduce: 1) Install KS with 1.7.4 build 2) Launch KS 3) Rotate the device to landscape result: Navbar remains full size (portrait size) expected: Navbar resizes based on orientation Notes: Using the orientation test (Phone>Orientation) and clicking "set landscape" forces the navbar to the thinner size. After this the navbar remains small despite orientation
