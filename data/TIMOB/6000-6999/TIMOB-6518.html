Description

We're currently getting reports from users who are manually swapping the simulator into retina mode that they can't do so anymore, due to the --retina flag support in ios-sim manually twiddling the application settings (due to ipad/iphone retina setting rendering problems). We need to disable this flag for the next release to ensure that people can still get to the retina sim.
