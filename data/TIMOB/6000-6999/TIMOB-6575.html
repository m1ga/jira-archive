I was expecting that. Steps to reproduce: 1) Create a new titanium mobile application 2) Include on the 57x57 and 72x72 icons required for a universal application 3) Package for distribution 4) In the organizer, click "verify" Result: Optional and not included icons are reported as errors Expected: Verification completes with no errors
