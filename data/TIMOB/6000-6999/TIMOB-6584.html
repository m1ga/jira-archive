Description

Description: In KS, on the Base UI>Views>Web Views>Youtube Video test, rotating the device while the video is loading first results in the status bar rotating without the UI (see attached image) then after the video is complete, the navbar is either drawn behind the status bar or is drawn with a gap between them. (See additional attached images). Steps to reproduce: 1) Install KS built with SDK 1.8.0.1 2) Navigate to Base UI>Views>Web Views>Youtube Video 3) Rotate the device while the video is loading Result: Status bar rotates without the UI, then rendering of the navbar is incorrect Note, This also occurs with KS and SDK 1.7.5 Expected: Either status bar doesn't rotate, or entire UI rotates preventing navbar rendering issues.
