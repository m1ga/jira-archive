Description

It appears that when we shut down a context, the associated XHR requests (and possibly other things) are not cleaned up in conjunction with them, or otherwise are being improperly managed. This behavior can be witnessed in Kitchen Sink: * KS -> Platform -> XHR -> setTimeout * Nav back via the top left button * Wait for the crash
