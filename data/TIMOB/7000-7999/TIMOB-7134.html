Description

Intermittent crash with log and stack traces (attached), ending with black screen and no automatic app restart. Happens every 10 minutes of KitchenSink testing on Android 4.0 device. Able to reproduce on Android 4.0 (5 times) and 2.3.6 (once) devices. Have not seen crash on Rhino or 1.8.0.1 so far.
