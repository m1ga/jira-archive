Description

Description: In KitchenSink iPad, while exercising the "Split View" test, I opened the modal window with "Show Modal" and then rotated the device from Portrait to upside-portrait. When I dismissed the modal, the UI was compressed and the status bar was on the side as if in landscape mode. See screenshot. Steps to reproduce: 1) Install KS iPad on iPad device 2) Launch KS iPad and choose "Split View - Plain" 3) hold iPad in portrait mode and press "Show Modal" 4) With the modal up, rotate the device 180 to upside-portrait 5) Dismiss the modal with "Dismiss modal" Result: UI does not fill screen, and status bar on side of screen as if in landscape mode. Note: This also occurs with 1.8.0.1
