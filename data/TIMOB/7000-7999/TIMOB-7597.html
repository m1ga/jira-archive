  1. Hieu Pham 2012-03-06 The delay seems to be a known Android issue. I notice if I keep clicking these 2 buttons very fast, I get error (-19, 0). This is b/c your app is creating multiple mediaPlayer objects at the same time (if you click very fast). You should try to create it once and re-use or clean up your previous media player before creating a new one. Reference article: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/5246898/mediaplayer-dies-android P.S: I tried on 1.8.0.1, 1.8.1, 1.8.2 and latest master and this issue persists.
