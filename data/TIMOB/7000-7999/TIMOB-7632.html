Comments

  1. Chris Barber 2016-08-24 The only thing you can do to somewhat limit which iOS version is to set the <min-ios-ver> setting in the <ios> section of the tiapp.xml. There's no way to limit the maximum iOS version. You can set UIRequiredDeviceCapabilities in the Info.plist to require features that can limit the devices you app runs on by requiring features such as "bluetooth-le". This won't guarantee a certain iOS version and it's up to the developer to figure out the possibilities.
