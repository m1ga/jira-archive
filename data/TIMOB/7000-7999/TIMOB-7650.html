Description

iOS has some inconsistencies with the other two platforms on how it handles tabs and newlines. Tabstop in iOS is only 1, whereas Android and Mobileweb have a tabstop of 2. A tabstop of 1 isn't very useful because it acts exactly like a space. If the text ends in a new-line, then the extra line is printed in Android and Mobile Web, but not in iOS.
