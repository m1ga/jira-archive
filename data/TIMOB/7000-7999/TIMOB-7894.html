Problem description

Splash screen is displayed also after bringing back the app from the background. Splash image should be displayed only after the app is launched.

Steps to reproduce

1. Install and open the default titanium studio app 2. Click on the home button 3. Launch again the app (both from app menu and from running app menu) Expected result: app is openend with no splash screen displayed. Result: app is opened with splash screen displayed.
