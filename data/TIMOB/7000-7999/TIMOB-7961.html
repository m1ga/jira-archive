  1. Allen Yeung 2012-03-14 I worked with Max a little on this, and I was unable to reproduce this issue after I installed a newer version of xcode.
  2. Max Stepanov 2012-03-15 User had both Xcode 4.0 (/Developer) and 4.2 (/Developer-4.3) installed. And Xcode 4.0 was used by default. Titanium does not support 4.0 since 1.8.0 release. Solution: switch to Xcode 4.2 and above. 
       sudo xcode-select /Developer-4.2
