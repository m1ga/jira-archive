Description

When the user's thumb is pressed on the button slider handle, and then the thumb is moved away, the custom thumb handle image disappears and is replaced by the default image. When the thumb is moved closer, the custom image reappears. This is an old issue. Steps to Reproduce: 1. Run Kitchen Sink > Controls > Slider > Basic. 2. On the top slider (there are two), press and hold the handle. 3. Move "thumb" (while keeping handle pressed) away from the handle. Expected Result: Custom image should remain. Actual Result: Custom image disappears.
