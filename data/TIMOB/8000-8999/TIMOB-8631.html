Description

Steps to reproduce: 1. Install and launch KS from 2_0_X branch 2. Go to Base UI > Views > Table Views > Touch Events 3. Press and hold on a row with "Row Label" under your finger 4. Slightly shift the weight of your finger up and down Actual: Contents in the row becomes invisible. If you press on the area where the label used to appear, the content appears again. Expected: Contents in the row should not become invisible. Note: Bug does not appear in sdk build 1.8.2
