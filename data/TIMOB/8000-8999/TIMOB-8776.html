Description

Expected label is not displayed when video is played from Kitchensink-PlayMovie-local. Steps to Reproduce: 1. Launch test case 2. Press the movie label 3. Wait until movie will finish playing two times and check xCode console Expected results: 1. Should see a movie playing and movie label should say "Do not try this at home" 2. The movie label should be changed on "mediaControlStyle= movieControlStyle= " 3. Should get an alert "Movie Completed!" In console should see info: [INFO] Event PlaybackState Fired: [INFO] activeMovie.endPlaybackTime: [INFO] activeMovie.playableDuration: " Actual Result: Movie plays successfully and console gets correct messages but labels are not displayed. Note: The scenario works fine with 1_8_x and 2_0_X.
