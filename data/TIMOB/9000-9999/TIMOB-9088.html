Description

Due to incompatibilities between iOS 3.x and 4.x+ with regards to audio streaming capability, iOS currently has two "AudioStreamer" classes which are interfaced with through a generic frontend. One of these, AudioStreamerBC, is for 3.x-compatibility purposes only and should be removed since it is dead code.
