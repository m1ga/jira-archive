Description

Issue

---- On iOS 5.0+ only, the sound produced by Ti.Media.AudioPlayer (with default settings) only comes out of the right channel of a headset. The behavior (stereo sound) is correct on the 4.3.5 simulator, so this may be an Apple bug or behavior change between major versions.

Testing

---- You're going to want a pair of headphones for this one. Kitchen Sink -> Phone -> Sound -> Remote Streaming -> "Start Streaming" *PASS:* Sound comes from both channels *FAIL:* Sound from right (or left) channel only
