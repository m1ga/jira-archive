  11. Chris Barber 2014-05-12 The emulator launch timeout is set to 2 minutes and the app launch timeout is set to 2 minutes. If your computer is slow, then you must increase these timeouts. 
        ti config android.emulatorStartTimeout 300000
    ti config android.appStartTimeout 300000
    You may also need to restart the ADB server: 
        adb kill-server
    adb start-server
