Description

Missing the 'toggle timeout' button & we do not get any progress indicator & messages like 'sending w/data' or 'sending w/o data'. Not a Regression, occurs with 2.0.2GA Steps to reproduce: IPhone 4.3.5: 1.Build kitchen sink for mobile web in titanium studio. 2.Type the ip address of your laptop into the mobile device's web browser.(safari for iphone & IE for windows phone). 3.Hit go or enter. 4.Kitchen sink will fire up in the browser. 5.Tap on platform tab. 6.Tap on the XHR table row. 7.Tap on the set timeout table row. 8.You will get a page with a 'toggle send' button on it & a text 'testing timeout with send data & without'. 9.Tapping on 'toggle send' gives us 'registered error:time passed = xxxx seconds.Where 'xxxx' can be any no. Windows Phone Mango: 1.We are not seeing the table row 'set timeout' as we are not able to scroll down the XHR page.Thus cant move ahead with the test. Actual result: 1.You will get a page only with a 'toggle send' button on it & a text 'testing timeout with send data & without' & missing the 'toggle timeout' button. 2.We are not seeing any progress bar & any messages like 'sending w/data' or 'sending w/o data'. Expected result: 1.Tapping on 'toggle timeout' should give us progress indicator and message "sending with data". After approximately 30-50 sec should get a message "Registered error: time passed=..." 2.Tapping on 'toggle send' should give us progress indicator and message "sending without data". After approximately 30-50 sec should get a message "Registered error: time passed=..."
