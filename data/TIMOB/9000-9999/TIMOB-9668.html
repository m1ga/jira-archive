Description

Missing "Toggle Switch in Navbar","Toggle Switch in Toolbar","Toggle Switch in Title" buttons from the page. Not a Regression, occurs with 2.0.2GA Steps to reproduce: 1.Build kitchen sink for mobile web in titanium studio. 2.Type the ip address of your laptop into the mobile device's web browser.(safari for iphone & IE for Windows Phone mango). 3.Hit go or enter. 4.Kitchen sink will fire up in the browser. 5.Tap on controls tab. 6.Tap on the switch table row. 7.You will see that the resulting page is missing the buttons "Toggle Switch in Navbar","Toggle Switch in Toolbar","Toggle Switch in Title". Actual result: 1."Toggle Switch in Navbar","Toggle Switch in Toolbar","Toggle Switch in Title" buttons are missing from the page. Expected result: 1.Tap on Press "Toggle Switch in Navbar" two times. 2.Tapping for the first time switch should appear in the nav bar & on the second time it should disappear. 3.Tap "Toggle Switch in Toolbar" two times. 4.Switch should appear in Toolbar after first pressing and disappear after second. 5.Tap"Toggle Switch in Title" two times. 6.Switch should appear in Title after first pressing and disappear after second.qe
