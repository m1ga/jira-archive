Description

The focus method on the textfield does not get focus on the textfield. Not a regression. This also occurs on 2.0.2 Steps to Reproduce: 1. Open Kitchen Application 2. Go to Control -> Textfield -> Events 3. Click on blur button 4. Click on Focus button Actual: The focus on the text field does not happen Expected: The text field should get focus on click of focus button
