  7. Vishal Duggal 2013-02-14 Note to QE: The difference in behavior you are seeing is due to the changes made for TIMOB-11866. Basically we moved the click event from the touchesBegan to touchesEnded/UITouchPhaseEnded section. Which means while previously the click event would fire immediately after the touchStart event it now fires after the touchEnd event. But for textArea (and I guess textField as well) the touchEnd field never fires (instead the touchCancel event fires). So you never see the click event. This is expected behavior.
